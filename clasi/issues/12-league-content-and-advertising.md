# League content + sidebar advertising

Wire in the business model: the League's own content is featured and the
site's sidebar advertising is the League's. This is the return that funds the
whole effort.

## Why

The League builds and runs the aggregator as goodwill; in exchange its events
appear in the directory and it owns the ad space. Without this, there's no
ROI on the work.

## Proposed scope

- **League events featured** — ensure jointheleague.org is a registered
  source and League events flow into the directory like any partner's.
- **League-owned sidebar ad slot** — a site requirement (coordinate with the
  `stem-ecosystem` repo): placement, format, and whether ads are static or
  rotate. Originates here as a business constraint.

## Sequence

Depends on: 02/05 (League as a source). The ad-slot piece is cross-repo with
the site; can proceed in parallel.
